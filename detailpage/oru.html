Integrated Well, Reservoir, and Facility Management in the Energy Sector

Course Overview: Integrated Well, Reservoir and Facility Management (WRFM) is a crucial approach in the energy industry that aims to optimize production, enhance asset value, and improve overall field performance by aligning the three domains of well, reservoir, and facility management, ensuring a seamless collaboration. WRFM is a structured and integrated approach that encompasses several critical components such as:

  • Well Management: Focuses on well performance, maintenance, and optimization.
  • Reservoir Management: Deals with understanding and predicting reservoir behaviour, fluid movement, and depletion strategies.
  • Facility Management: Involves operating and maintaining production facilities efficiently.

This course provides a comprehensive understanding of managing oil and gas assets efficiently. Participants will learn about integrated approaches to optimize well performance, reservoir management techniques, and facility operations. The subject discussions include reservoir modeling, production optimization, maintenance strategies, and environmental considerations. Through case studies and simulations, participants will gain practical insights into decision-making processes across the entire value chain, ensuring sustainable resource development and maximizing operational efficiency.
